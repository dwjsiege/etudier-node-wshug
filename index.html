Why it matters

Climate change, pollution, and habitat loss threaten people and ecosystems. Collective action ‚Äî from households to governments ‚Äî can shift the trajectory.

Practical actions you can take

Reduce Waste

  • Bring reusable bags, bottle, and coffee cup.
  • Buy less single-use plastic; choose products with minimal packaging.
  • Compost food scraps to return nutrients to soil.

Save Energy

  • Switch to LED bulbs and energy-efficient appliances.
  • Unplug devices when not in use and use smart power strips.
  • Improve insulation to lower heating/cooling demand.

Green Transportation

  • Walk, bike, carpool, or use public transit when possible.
  • Consider hybrid or electric vehicles for long-term replacement.
  • Plan errands to reduce driving time.

Protect Nature

Support native plants, avoid pesticides, and create wildlife-friendly spaces (e.g., birdhouses, pollinator gardens).

Consume Consciously

Choose durable goods, repair instead of replace, buy second-hand, and prefer companies with transparent sustainability practices.

Learn more ‚Äî quick facts

Climate

Human activities increase greenhouse gases, warming the planet. Reducing emissions slows impacts on weather, sea levels, and ecosystems.

Biodiversity

Healthy ecosystems provide food, clean water, and services. Protecting habitats preserves species and resilience.

Pollution

Air, water, and soil pollution harm health and ecosystems. Reducing chemical and plastic pollution improves quality of life.
